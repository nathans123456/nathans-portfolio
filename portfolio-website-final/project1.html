This is some of my portrait photography skills, two portraits are outside with naturally lighting while the other is in doors in a photography studio using studio lighting. The two pictures outside are on college campus during a nice sunny day which we took advantage of and got some really nice lighting.

The image in the studio was for a college assignment were we had to take multiple images of different people in the photography studio, out of all the images I took this one was definitely one of my favourites.
