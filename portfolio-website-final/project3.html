These images were taking as part as an assignment for college, the assignment was to take images and document a story with them. My story was based on my Grandad and the photos were taking of the house he grew up in and areas around there that all have a significant stroy to him.

I put a black and white effect on all images to show that they represent an old story and I think adding that gave them a whole different feel to them, there was more photos that I took put this were a selection of my favourite.
