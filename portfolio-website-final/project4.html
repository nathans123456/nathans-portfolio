Off White Toolbox
Off White Toolbox
Off White Toolbox
Previous Next

When covid first hit I was doing a module in college where we would have been taking images in a studio of products and making almost an advertisement for the item. Of course, with covid we were at home and had to continue the module from home. At the time I hasn’t even got my own camera I was using cameras from college, so all of these shots were taking with an iPhone.

Of course, because I was at home I had to get creative for the setup I was using, I used some white wood I had from a new drawer I got that I hadn’t put together yet, the screws also all came from the drawer and the item I captured was an Off White toolbox which I thought was cool and something different.
