Haunting of Hill House
Haunting of Hill House
Rick and Morty
Previous Next

This was an assignment for college to create a book cover for a book that already exists, a tv show, movie or anything really. The goal of the assignment was to create the cover using typography as the main subject. The Haunting of Hill house cover and ack cover was part of my final piece that I was really happy with.

The Rick and Morty cover was a concept I did out but I was on two minds weather to go ahead with the Rick and Morty or the Haunting of Hill House as I did really like the Rick and Morty cover too.
